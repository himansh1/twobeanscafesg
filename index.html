More Than a Café — It's Home 🏠

Two Beans is a cosy home café born out of a neighbourly friendship and a shared love of good coffee. Started by two neighbours, Sophia/Ces and Tia, this little corner exists for those who crave more than just a cup — they crave connection.

Whether you pull up for a quick takeaway or stay for hours with Mr. Brooklyn and Mr. Dango by your side, you'll always leave with a full cup and a fuller heart. That's the Two Beans promise. ☕🐾

We believe the best barista is one who knows your order — and your dog's name.

Meet Our Fluffy Residents 🐾

They don't bite — but they will absolutely steal your croissant

Mr. Brooklyn

Golden Retriever 🌟

The ultimate host. Mr. Brooklyn will greet you at the door, nap dramatically under your table, and somehow make your coffee taste better just by being there. He's the reason half our regulars keep coming back.

Chief Nap Officer ✓
🐾 🐾 🐾
🐕

Mr. Dango

Shiba Inu 🍡

Sweet as the mochi he's named after, with just enough Shiba attitude to keep things interesting. Mr. Dango will judge your coffee order silently but still sit with you on your worst days. A true mood booster with flair.

Head of Vibes ✓
